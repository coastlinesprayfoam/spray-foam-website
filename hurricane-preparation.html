🛡️ How Spray Foam Protects Your Home from Hurricanes

Spray foam insulation provides multiple layers of protection beyond traditional insulation

💪

Structural Reinforcement

Adds 300% structural strength to walls and roof systems by creating a monolithic bond with building materials.

  • Prevents wall separation during high winds
  • Reduces roof uplift forces
  • Strengthens connection points
  • Creates unified structural system
🌊

Water Damage Prevention

Complete moisture barrier prevents water infiltration during storm surge and heavy rains.

  • Seals all cracks and gaps
  • Prevents mold and mildew growth
  • Protects electrical systems
  • Reduces flood damage restoration costs
💨

Wind Resistance

Superior air sealing prevents pressure differentials that can cause catastrophic roof failure.

  • Eliminates air leaks completely
  • Prevents pressure buildup
  • Reduces wind-driven rain infiltration
  • Maintains structural integrity
