Our Story

Founded in 2008 with a mission to help Florida families and businesses achieve energy independence through superior insulation solutions.

Coastline Spray Foam was born out of frustration with Florida's skyrocketing energy costs and the inadequate insulation solutions available to homeowners and businesses. Our founder, a Florida native, experienced firsthand the struggle of keeping homes comfortable while managing astronomical energy bills.

What started as a small, local operation continues to serve Central Florida with quality spray foam insulation services. We've helped several happy customers save on energy costs while making their homes and businesses more comfortable.

Today, we're proud to be Florida's premier spray foam insulation experts, serving Orlando, Melbourne, Cocoa, Titusville, Palm Coast, and all of Central Florida with the same commitment to quality and customer satisfaction that started it all.
