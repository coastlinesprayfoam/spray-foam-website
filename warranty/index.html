Our Service Approach

What's Covered

Our service commitment includes:

  • Material Defects: Any defects in the spray foam material itself
  • Installation Issues: Problems resulting from improper installation
  • Performance Failures: Failure to meet specified R-values or air sealing performance
  • Adhesion Problems: Spray foam separating from surfaces
  • Settling or Shrinkage: Any settling or shrinkage of the installed foam
  • Moisture Infiltration: Moisture problems caused by foam failure

What's Not Covered

This warranty does not cover:

  • Damage from natural disasters (hurricanes, floods, earthquakes)
  • Damage from building modifications not performed by us
  • Normal wear and tear from extreme age (beyond reasonable building life)
  • Damage from pest infestation or rodents
  • Issues caused by building settling or structural movement
  • Damage from improper maintenance or neglect

Warranty Service Process

  1. Contact Us: Call (321) 652-7465 or email info@coastlinesprayfoam.com
  2. Inspection: We'll schedule a free inspection promptly
  3. Assessment: Our technician will assess the issue and determine coverage
  4. Repair: If covered, we'll complete repairs at no cost to you
  5. Follow-up: We'll follow up to ensure your complete satisfaction

Transferability

Our warranty is transferable to new property owners with the following conditions:

  • Written notification to Coastline Spray Foam within 30 days of property transfer
  • Proof of original installation and warranty documentation
  • Property inspection by our technician (free of charge)
  • No modifications to the insulation system by unauthorized parties

Maintenance Requirements

To maintain warranty coverage:

  • Do not modify or remove spray foam without our authorization
  • Maintain proper ventilation as recommended
  • Address any building leaks or moisture issues promptly
  • Allow annual inspections if requested (rare, but may be necessary)
